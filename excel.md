# day to day opperations
- Page layout will adjust how the page prints
- to adjust whole colomn hover mouse over letter or number will you see black down arrow and then click
  - right click to format cell 
  - under conditional formatting you can adjust the color scales (I prefer bars)
  - to insert you will need to right click on the collomn to the right and then choose insert
- to do percentages in the cell, select the cell and then the percentage symbol
- right click on the cell to sort
- the funtion area looks like a sideways m. it has basic formulas you may want to use
- excel jet website is a great online resource
- add comments is indicated by a purple/pink tag. to add notes right click on the cell. you can tag people here
  - add a note is indicated by a red tag
